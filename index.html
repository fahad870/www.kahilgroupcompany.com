About Kahil Group

Building sustainable businesses for regional and global growth.

Kahil Group Companies operates across multiple industries with a mission of creating economic value, empowering communities, and building strong enterprise systems.


Our divisions focus on bakery manufacturing, investments, transport logistics, and social impact initiatives that support long-term development.


Kahil Bakery Company, Kahil Investments Group, Kahil Transportation Company, and Kahil Charity Foundation are all proudly united under Kahil Group Companies.

Our Business

Kahil Group Companies is home to four strong enterprises that deliver bakery products, investment solutions, transportation services, and charitable support.

Kahil Bakery Company

Produces Hill Bread, Hill Snacks, and Hill Wheat Flour with a focus on freshness, quality, and customer satisfaction.

Kahil Investments Group

Supports strategic growth through capital partnerships, business development, and regional economic opportunities.

Kahil Transportation Company

Provides reliable transport and logistics services for goods distribution across markets and supply chains.

Kahil Charity Foundation

Invests in community empowerment, education, and sustainable social initiatives for local development.
