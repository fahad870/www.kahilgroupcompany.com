Our Business Portfolio

Kahil Group Companies is composed of four strong enterprises working together to deliver bakery, investment, transportation, and charitable services.

Companies under Kahil Group

Each business delivers a specialized service while sharing a common mission of growth, quality, and community impact.

Kahil Bakery Company

Produces Hill Bread, Hill Snacks, and Hill Wheat Flour. Our bakery business focuses on quality ingredients, consistent production, and customer satisfaction.

Kahil Investments Group

Provides strategic investment support, business funding, and partnership development across the region to help companies grow and scale responsibly.

Kahil Transportation Company

Offers reliable logistics, delivery, and transport services that ensure products move safely and efficiently throughout our markets.

Kahil Charity Foundation

Supports community initiatives, education, and sustainable development programs designed to improve lives and create positive social change.
